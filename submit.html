Submissions

BESË Review invites submissions that engage seriously with global affairs, human rights, public policy, and the structures that shape public life.

The journal seeks work that demonstrates clarity of thought, analytical depth, and a sustained engagement with real-world issues. It prioritises writing that examines overlooked realities and approaches complexity with precision, structure, and intellectual seriousness.

Submissions that are purely opinion-based, descriptive, or lacking in critical inquiry are unlikely to be accepted.

Editorial Focus

  • Global affairs and international relations
  • Human rights and social justice
  • Public policy and governance
  • Labour, inequality, and public ethics
  • Serious student essays rooted in research and analysis

How to Submit

Submissions should be sent by email and include the article, the author’s name, and a brief biographical note.

All submissions are reviewed editorially. Acceptance is based on quality, relevance, and alignment with the journal’s focus.
